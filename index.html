Hi, I'm Smty Pradhan — a Business Strategy and Analytics professional based in New York City.

My work sits at the intersection of innovation, impact, and insight. I'm passionate about using data to tell compelling stories that drive business growth.

Over the past three years, I've worked with over 100+ startups, leading the marketing, community-building, and early-stage investment portfolio of a business incubator. I have also led cross-functional programs from concept to execution, driving end-to-end solutions across startup ecosystem, including programs with Meta (XR Startup Program) and Wadhwani Foundation (Startup School). This experience has equipped me with a deep understanding of the startup ecosystem and the skills to navigate its challenges.

I'm excited to leverage my expertise to help businesses and entrepreneurs succeed in today's fast-paced and dynamic environment. Let's connect and explore how we can work together to create innovative solutions and drive business success.
